How it works

Simple, clear support without pressure or confusing technical language.

1

Reach out

Call, text, or send a message and let us know what’s going on. No technical details needed.

2

We ask a few questions, explain the easiest next step, and decide whether phone help, email, or an in-home visit makes sense.

3

Get help your way

We provide patient support by phone, text, email, video, or in person, depending on the problem and what feels most comfortable.
